Le Magnolia Café

Explore

Nestled in the heart of the deep South, you'll find "Le Magnolia Café," a charming country French restaurant that serves up the most delectable dishes this side of the Mississippi. With its cozy, rustic ambiance and warm Southern hospitality, you'll feel right at home from the moment you step inside.

At Le Magnolia Café, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to create our mouth-watering dishes. Our chefs are experts at blending traditional French cooking techniques with classic Southern flavors to create a truly unique dining experience.

Start off your meal with our famous shrimp and grits, made with plump, juicy shrimp and creamy, stone-ground grits. Or, try our savory crawfish étouffée, a rich and flavorful dish that will transport you straight to the heart of Louisiana.

For your main course, we recommend our succulent chicken and sausage jambalaya, slow-cooked to perfection with the perfect blend of herbs and spices. Or, try our tender beef bourguignon, served with a side of buttery mashed potatoes.

And don't forget to save room for dessert! Our homemade pecan pie is a true Southern classic, made with sweet, gooey filling and a flaky, buttery crust. Or, indulge in our decadent bread pudding, served with a generous drizzle of warm caramel sauce.

At Le Magnolia Café, we take pride in providing our guests with an unforgettable dining experience. Come join us for a taste of the deep South, with a French twist!
